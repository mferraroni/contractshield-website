Independent Contractor Status

Consultant is an independent contractor, not an employee. Client does not direct work methods, hours, or tools. No benefits, payroll taxes, or employment protections apply.

Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice. Client pays for all work completed to date. Any prepaid retainer for unperformed work is refunded pro-rata.

Non-Solicitation

Client will not directly solicit, hire, or contract any members of Consultant's team for 12 months after engagement ends.

✓

Non-Compete (narrow)

Consultant will not provide identical services to Client's direct competitors during the engagement term. Consultant retains the right to serve other clients in other industries.

✓

Work Product Ownership

Client owns the custom deliverables produced for this engagement upon full payment. Consultant retains pre-existing methodologies, frameworks, and tools used to create them.
